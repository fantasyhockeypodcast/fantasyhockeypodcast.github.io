Summary

Your 2020 fantasy hockey preview for the North (aka Canadian) division! We discuss all the fantasy relevant players on the Boston Bruins, Buffalo Sabres, New York Islanders, New York Rangers, Philadelphia Flyers, Pittsburgh Penguins and the Washington Capitals. We also answer the important questions such as, who's poised to breakout, who's going to fall back to earth, and what players should I be placing on my watchlist immediately after my draft ends?
