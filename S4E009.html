First Fantasy Hockey Mock Draft of 2019 on Yahoo | Fantasy Hockey Podcast

Summary

Our first mock drafts on Yahoo for the 2019 Fantasy Hockey Season! We did a 12-person and 10-person standard H2H cats in this show.
Many have asked us how to join these mock drafts and the answer is by become a patron! If you sign up for Patreon you'll get priority in our mock drafts before the public.
This draft was a 12-team H2H standard cats mock draft on Yahoo. It was a positional draft (2C, 2LW, 2RW, 4D, 2G, 4 Bench) which made things interesting.
Standard Yahoo Categories are G, A, PPP, SOG, HIT, +/-, W, SO, SV%, GAA
Every weekend leading up to the season we're doing live Fantasy Hockey mock drafts on different platforms to get you ready for the season. We'll be streaming them via twitch and uploading on YouTube. Join our discord to chat with over 300 fantasy hockey nuts + us!

Watch it again!
