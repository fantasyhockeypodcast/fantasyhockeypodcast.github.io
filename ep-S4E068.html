Summary

In this episode we prep you for your fantasy hockey playoffs. We walk you through the trade deadline with a slew of trade targets and players to avoid. We also discuss trade strategies depending on where you're at in the table. Should you be stocking up in a keeper league or should you play it safe? Lastly, we walk you through the playoff schedule and talk through who has good/bad schedules.
