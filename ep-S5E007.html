Summary

Your 2020 fantasy hockey preview for the North (aka Canadian) division! We discuss all the fantasy relevant players on the Calgary Flames, Edmonton Oilers, Montreal Canadiens, Ottawa Senators, Toronto Maple Leafs, Vancouver Canucks and the Winnipeg Jets. We also answer the important questions such as, who's poised to breakout, who's going to fall back to earth, and what players should I be placing on my watchlist immediately after my draft ends?
