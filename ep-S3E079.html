Episode S3E079 - First Annual NHL Razzie Awards | Fantasy Hockey Podcast

Summary

We're excited to bring you the first ever NHL Razzies Awards Show this year. An awards show where we hand over the spotlight to the worst of the worst! This all came about because we believe that the players that broke your hearts and made you throw your beer bottle through your living room window out of frustration should get an award too. The best part is that there's exciting categories for all, so nobody can escape the academy's wrath. We're thrilled you could join us on this fine evening and we sure hope you enjoy the show.
