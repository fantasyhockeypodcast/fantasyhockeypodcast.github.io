Summary

Our first ever mock draft and the first one of the 2019 season! The stats counted for this league were G, A, PIM, SOG, PPP, HIT, BLCK, W, SV%, and GAA. Gambi is picking from the 8th position while Brandon got the lucky (sometimes unlucky?) 1st overall pick.

Every weekend leading up to the season we're doing live 2019 Fantasy Hockey mock drafts on different platforms to get you ready for the season. We'll be streaming them via twitch and uploading on Youtube. Be sure to become a patron to get first dibs to draft with us in the mock drafts and join our discord to chat with over 250 fantasy hockey nuts + us!
