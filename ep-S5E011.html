Summary

We go over lines for every team from training camp with the incredible duo from Keeping Karlsson in our second ever crossover episode! We discuss what early roster moves or line deployments might mean for some players. Do you rush to pick them up, do they jump in your draft rank? We answer all the questions. On this show we discuss the East and Central divisions. Check out Keeping Karlsson for the discussion on the North and South divisions.
